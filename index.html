Welcome to the Colorado Springs Education Group

We are a group of former and current educators, parents, and concerned community members that cares deeply for public education.

Mission Statement

To reimagine, cultivate, and elevate safe, resourced, accessible, and joyful public schools for all kids, teachers, parents, and our community at large by growing power among Colorado Springs educators, parents, and students in order to win the contracts, school board elections, and policy changes necessary to improve working conditions of public school educators and learning conditions for students.
